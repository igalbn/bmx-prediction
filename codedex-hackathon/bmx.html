Paris 2024 Olympics: prediction of winners in Men's BMX freestyle

Intro

This sport was added to Olympics program, only during the last games in Tokyo. The data was collected manually from online resources. The prediction done based on data analysis.

Prediction description

The prediction is based mainly on the average score of best result in final of the two qualification events in Shanghai and Budapest. The weight that is given to every column during calculation of final prediction score is based on personal estimation.
